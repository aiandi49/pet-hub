Built in Tucson · Open to All

PetHub started the way most real things do — out of a genuine problem. In the 85710 zip code on Tucson's East Side, pets go missing all the time. Neighbors want to help but have no easy way to connect. Lost pet flyers on telephone poles in 2024 felt like the wrong answer.

So we built PetConnect first. A real-time, GPS-powered lost & found network where any neighbor can post — no account required. Within weeks, people were using it. That told us something.

We kept going. PawMatch came next — because lonely dogs deserve playmates, and owners deserve community. Then Paw Pack — because pet owners love showing off their animals, and the internet loves voting on cute pets.

Three apps. One hub. Zero cost — always.

PetHub is built and operated by VTREI LLC, a small team based right here in Tucson, AZ 85710. We're not backed by venture capital. We're not trying to sell your data. We're just dog people who wanted something better for our neighborhood — and decided to build it for yours too.

Every city. Every pet. Every owner. That's the mission.
