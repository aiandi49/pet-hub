The Platform

Three apps. One mission.

01 — Core App
Live on GitHub

PetConnect

"Your neighbor found your dog before you even knew it was gone."

The anchor of the PetHub ecosystem. A real-time emergency network for lost pets, found pets, and stray sightings — in your neighborhood, your city, anywhere. P.A.W.S. alerts fire like an Amber Alert the moment someone posts nearby. GPS location capture, voice-to-text posting, photo uploads, and a verified claim flow so only the real owner can mark a pet as reunited.

🔴 P.A.W.S. Alerts 📍 GPS Location 🎤 Voice Posting 🐾 Lost · Found · Stray ✅ Verified Claims 📱 Mobile First

02 — Social App
Live on GitHub

PawMatch

"Tinder for pets — swipe right on your dog's next best friend."

The social layer of PetHub. Dog owners anywhere create profiles for their pets and get matched with compatible playdate partners nearby. Swipe through pups, see energy levels and size compatibility, and connect with other owners for meetups at your favorite local park. Built with a dark, sophisticated design — nothing kiddish here.

💚 Swipe Matching 📸 Pet Profiles 🗺 Your Local Parks ⚡ Energy Matching 🐕 Size Compatibility 🔒 Safety Board
💚
↗
03 — Entertainment App
Live on Vercel

Paw Pound

"Hot or Not — but make it pets. Vote, rank, and crown the world's cutest."

The fun layer of PetHub. Post your pet, get rated by other owners, climb the Hot Board leaderboard, and build streaks. Real dog photos via live API, confetti animations on big moments, and a Live Votes feed so you can watch the community weigh in in real time. It's the app that gets people talking — and sharing — and coming back daily.

🔥 Hot or Not Voting 🏆 Live Leaderboard 📸 Real Dog Photos 🎉 Confetti Moments 🔁 Daily Streaks ⚡ Live Feed
