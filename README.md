# 🐾 PetHub — Tucson's Free Pet Community Platform

> **Three apps. One mission. Every pet connected, protected, and loved.**  
> Built by Lamar Myers · VTREI LLC · Tucson, Arizona 85710

## The Platform

PetHub is the home for three free community apps built specifically for Tucson's East Side (85710). Whether you've lost a pet, want to find your dog a playdate, or just want to show off your best friend — there's an app for that.

---

## The Apps

### 🚨 [PetConnect](./petconnect) — Lost & Found Alerts
Tucson's emergency network for lost pets, found pets, and stray sightings.
Real-time P.A.W.S. Alerts · GPS location · Voice posting · Verified claims
**→ [Launch PetConnect](https://pethub-tucson.github.io/petconnect)**

---

### 💚 [PawMatch](./pawmatch) — Dog Playdate Matching
Tinder for dogs in 85710. Create a profile, swipe, match, and set up a playdate at Brandi Fenton or Himmel Park.
Size & energy matching · Safe meetup spots · Mobile first
**→ [Launch PawMatch](https://pethub-tucson.github.io/pawmatch)**

---

### 🏆 [Paw Pound](./paw-pound) — Community Pet Ratings *(Beta)*
Hot or Not for pets. Vote on Tucson's cutest animals, climb the leaderboard, build streaks.
Live voting · Real dog photos · Daily streaks · Confetti moments
**→ [Launch Paw Pound](https://pethub-tucson.github.io/paw-pound)**

---

## Why We Built This

Tucson's East Side is a tight-knit community. Every week, neighbors post in Facebook groups: *"Has anyone seen my dog?"* — and the community scrambles to help with no real tools.

PetHub gives that community the infrastructure it deserves. Built by a neighbor, for neighbors. No ads, no subscriptions, no nonsense.

---

## Tech Stack

All apps are built as progressive web apps — HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Supabase.  
No frameworks. No build steps. Open on your phone and use immediately.
